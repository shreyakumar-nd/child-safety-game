Online Safety Learning Game

Get equipped to keep the internet safe for young people.

These scenarios reflect the choices that children, young people, parents, and caregivers are presented with every day. They also show the risks those choices can entail. How would you proceed?

Note: The situations replicate ways in which unsuspecting young people online are put at risk or in harm's way. The scenarios capture elements of trust-building that can later be abused, potential exploitation, and online harm. Younger participants should use this informative tool with informed and trusted adults nearby.
